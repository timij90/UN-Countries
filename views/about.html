About

Everything you want to know about me - the designer & developer.


Hey there! My name is Jacobs Timi Uba and I'm currently studying Computer Programming & Analysis at Seneca Polytechnic.

This website was created for the WEB322 course which displays a vibrant and adaptable design. Inside, you'll find pages dedicated to different countries, a brief overview of who I am, and a custom 404 error page.
To ensure a smooth user experience, I've made the site adaptable to various devices. The landing page features links to different country regions in a dropdown menu, and the "about" section explains the project and tells a bit about myself.
I've also added a touch of style using Tailwind CSS and daisyui for a polished and professional look.

Outside of school, I love to play tennis and listen to music.
